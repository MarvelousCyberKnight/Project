About

  • NBKR Institute of Science and Technology is a private autonomous institution established in the year 1979. It is also known as NBKRIST. The college is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Anantapur. The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has accredited the institute with an ‘A’ grade. It has been granted ‘College with Potential for Excellence (CPE)’ status by the University Grants Commission (UGC). The campus of NBKRIST is spread over an area of 184 acres and it has a total of 204 faculty members. As per the official website of the college, its mission is to develop effective learning skills enabling students to pick up critical thinking thus crafting them professionally fit and ethically strong.

  • The college offers B.Tech, M.Tech. and Ph.D courses. All the Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) courses of the institute are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). NBKRIST accepts AP EAMCET scores for admission to B.Tech courses. The college offers its courses in full-time mode. To provide industrial training to the students, the institute has collaborations with numerous corporations. The training and placement cell of NBKR Institute of Science and Technology offers a large number of placement and internship opportunities to the students. The campus of the institute has modern and upgraded facilities & infrastructure for students and faculty members.

Location

  • Our institution is located at Vidyanagar Kota, S.P.S.R, Andhra Pradesh.

  • The nearest airport to the college is Tirupati International Airport. It is located 82.5 km away from the campus.

  • With a distance of 25.7 km, Pedapariya Railway Station is the nearest railway station to the institute.

  • Our institution is situated at a distance of 4.4 km away from the Nayudupet Bus Stand.
