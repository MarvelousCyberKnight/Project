Training & Placement Cell

A separate Training & Placement Cell is existing in the college and one of the senior members is acting as head of the Training & Placement Cell and he is assisted by one experienced Placement Officer and five trainers.

One faculty member from each Engineering Department is deputed as Training & Placement Office Coordinator to maintain the rapport between the department and the Training & Placement Cell. A separate wing is allotted to the Training & Placement cell.

Training Programs for GATE and Technical Training in C, Java & Python are being conducted regularly to the final and Pre-final students of all classes along with Campus Recruitment Training Programs to impart competitive skills, communication skills, mock Interviews and Group discussions techniques.

Around 30 companies from both software and core visit the college every year. The average salary during the present academic year is 3.05 Lakhs with highest being 6.00 Lakhs per annum.

OBJECTIVES

  1. To showcase NBKRIST as the placement destination for all the corporate companies.
  2. To impart the technical and soft skill training programs to enhance the employability skills of the students.
  3. To conduct more Industry-Institute-Interaction.
  4. To keep self-targets for increasing the number of Companies to be visited ( previous Placement number).
  5. To encourage students to participate in paper presentations, seminars, workshops and conferences.
  6. Scheduling Campus Training programs from 1st year to prepare students to cater according to the corporate needs & requirements.
  7. To invite corporate people to set up their “Centre of Excellence” in the college.
