Admission Procedure


NBKR Institute of Science & Technology offers 4 year Undergraduate Programme in Engineering (B.Tech),2 year Postgraduate Programme in Engineering (M.Tech) and Doctoral Program in Engineering(PhD), The admission into the B.Tech courses except Category-B Seats is through a State-Level Entrance Test called "Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test" (EAPCET) conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

70% seats are alloted by the Convenor, EAMCET and the Remaining 30%(Category-B)are filled by the apsche based on the Rank obtained in JEE Mains or EAMCET or Intermediate marks. Sanctioned Intake for the The College from the Academic Year 2021 - 2022 :: 960
